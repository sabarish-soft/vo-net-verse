# Von Neumann: Rise of the Network

## Core Vision

A galactic civilization-building game inspired by:

* Bobiverse
* Rise of Nations
* Dyson Sphere Program
* Civilization

The game focuses on:

* Exploration
* Expansion
* Resource management
* Technological advancement
* Self-replication

There are no enemies in the initial versions.

The challenge comes from:

* Limited resources
* Time
* Logistics
* Strategic decisions
* Competing priorities

The player experiences the growth of a civilization from a single probe to a galaxy-spanning network.

---

# Rise of Nations Inspired Systems

## 1. Galactic Ages

Just like Rise of Nations.

The civilization advances through Ages.

Each Age unlocks new capabilities.

### Age 1 - Pioneer Age

Starting phase.

Unlocks:

* Basic mining
* Basic probes
* Local factories

Goal:

Reach 5 colonized systems.

---

### Age 2 - Expansion Age

Unlocks:

* Faster probes
* Resource transport routes
* System specialization

Goal:

Reach 20 systems.

---

### Age 3 - Automation Age

Unlocks:

* Autonomous colonies
* Automated logistics
* Large factories

Goal:

Reach 50 systems.

---

### Age 4 - Stellar Age

Unlocks:

* Orbital habitats
* Solar energy harvesting
* Mega factories

Goal:

Reach 100 systems.

---

### Age 5 - Dyson Age

Unlocks:

* Dyson Swarms
* Massive energy production
* Interstellar manufacturing

Goal:

Control major sectors.

---

### Age 6 - Singularity Age

Unlocks:

* Self-designing probes
* Instant communications
* AI specialization

---

### Age 7 - Transcendence Age

Unlocks:

* Consciousness networks
* Intergalactic expansion
* Civilization victory

* Factories
* Habitats

---

### Energy

Generated from stars.

Used for:

* Manufacturing
* Communication
* Megastructures

---

### Knowledge

Generated by research centers.

* Advancing ages
* Unlocking technologies

The player constantly decides:

Expand?
Or research?

# 3. Territorial Influence

Inspired by National Borders.

Every colony creates an influence radius.

Influence determines:

* Resource access
* Expansion efficiency
* Logistics efficiency

Visualization:

Animated expanding circles around stars.

When circles overlap:

Network efficiency improves.

This creates satisfying visual growth.

# 6. Trade Network System

Instead of military supply lines.

# MVP Recommendation

Version 1 should include:

1. Galaxy Map
2. Resource Gathering
3. Colonization
4. Age Progression
5. Research
6. System Specialization
7. LocalStorage Save

Do NOT build:

* Combat
* Diplomacy
* Aliens
* Probe personalities

Yet.

The goal is to first create the "one more turn" feeling that Rise of Nations mastered.

The player should always feel they are one discovery, one age advancement, or one major project away from becoming dramatically more powerful.
